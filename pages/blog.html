Blog: How Technology Helps Growing Food in Cities

These simple articles talk about how phones, apps, and online groups help people live in a way that cares for the earth in Pokhara and Nepal.

About Technology in Urban Gardening

Smart Ways to Grow Plants in Small Homes

Apps on your phone and sensors that check soil wetness help people in Pokhara take care of plants on their balconies, even when they are busy.

Positive Impact of Technology

Saving Water with Smart Tools

Cheap timers and slow water systems connected to the internet use less water and help herbs grow well on rooftops near Phewa Lake.

Negative Impact of Technology

E-waste and Overconsumption

Too much dependency on new devices can increase e-waste. We should prioritize repairable tools and long-term products.
