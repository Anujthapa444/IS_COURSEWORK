Grow Your Own Food in Pokhara: UrbanSprout Nepal

A student project for CC4057NP focused on sustainable urban living, balcony and kitchen gardening, and local eco-friendly gardening products in Pokhara valley.

What is UrbanSprout?

UrbanSprout helps urban residents in Nepal, especially Pokhara, to start balcony and kitchen gardening while choosing plastic-free and locally made gardening products from Lakeside and nearby local markets.

Gardening Product Store
